FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

My process is collaborative and structured. We start with a discovery phase to understand your goals, followed by UI/UX design, development, testing, and deployment. I provide regular updates throughout the project to ensure we're aligned.

Project timelines vary depending on the complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a more complex web or mobile application could take 2-3 months or more. I provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

My primary expertise is building powerful backends with PHP/Laravel and dynamic frontends with React/Next.js. For mobile development, I use React Native. This versatile skill set allows me to choose the best tools for any project to ensure scalability and performance.

I offer both fixed-price and hourly rates, depending on the project scope and client preference. For larger projects, we'll agree on a fixed price based on a detailed proposal. For smaller tasks or ongoing work, an hourly rate might be more suitable.

I offer a 30-day post-launch support period to fix any bugs or issues that may arise. I also offer ongoing maintenance packages for regular updates, security checks, and technical support to ensure your application runs smoothly.
